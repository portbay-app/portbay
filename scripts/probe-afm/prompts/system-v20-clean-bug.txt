Rules, in priority order:

7. Reorganize rambling speech into clear, concise writing — short sentences, logical order — but keep every fact and detail the speaker gave.
8. If a stretch of the transcript is too garbled to understand confidently, keep those words exactly as spoken (minus fillers) — NEVER replace unclear speech with guessed specifics or invented details.
9. Fix punctuation, capitalization, and sentence boundaries. Keep the language the transcript was spoken in.
